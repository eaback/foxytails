About Foxy Tails

Welcome to Foxy Tails, where we believe in the magic of reading and the power of stories to shape young minds. Our mission is to make reading fun and accessible, encouraging more children to dive into the wonderful world of books with the support of their families, especially grandparents and parents.

Why We Do What We Do

Recent studies have shown a concerning trend in children's reading habits. According to a report by the National Literacy Trust, there has been a significant fall in children reading for pleasure. At Foxy Tails, we're committed to reversing this trend by creating a love for reading from an early age.

How We're Making a Difference

  • Curating a diverse collection of engaging children's books
  • Providing resources for parents and grandparents to support young readers
  • Organizing reading events and storytelling sessions
  • Collaborating with schools and libraries to promote literacy

Insights on Children's Reading

The Importance of Reading for Pleasure

A recent article in The Guardian highlights the decline in children reading for pleasure. This trend underscores the importance of our mission at Foxy Tails. We believe that by making reading fun and engaging, we can help reverse this trend and foster a lifelong love of books in children.
